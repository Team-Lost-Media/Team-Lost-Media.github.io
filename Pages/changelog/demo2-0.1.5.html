Wega And The Lost Media - Demo 2 Version 0.1.5 - Changelog

New Stuff

Added Multiplayer support. Play local co-op with another player!

Added 3 new playable characters. Play as Urio (finally), Halo 2 Brother (unlockable), and a third, mysterious character... (you know who already.)
Added character select menu

Added 3 new "Bonus Levels" with their own media and jpeg coins. Bonus Levels are optional bonuses you can play after clearing World 1.
Added water physics

Added a new weapon: The Sigma Blaster. Wield the power of Golden Sigma after clearing World 1!

Added multiple save support. Play on 4 different save files for repeat playthroughs!
0.1.0 (Demo 1) saves are not compatible with 0.1.5. (Demo 2)

Added 4 new costumes and 1 new file extension.
Added tutorials for the basic controls as well as the sigma blaster controls.
Added Community art slideshow to the title screen if you wait long enough.
Added 8 new songs.
Added 7 new achievements.

Updates

Updated various menus to look and feel better. (Pause menu, Shop menu, Main menu, Options menu.)
Updated Keybinds and controller support. No more do you have to tediously bind a controller manually!
Updated VFX to occasionally look better.
Updated Golden Sigma's boss sprites.
Updated menus to be usable with only the mouse.
Updated various level dialogues.
Updated player sprites. All characters now have idle animations.
Updated misc sprites.
Updated the length of Evil Coal Chase.
Updated the background of 1-4.
Updated credits menu.
Updated Godot version to 4.5.

Fixed Misaligned captions.
Fixed the in-game speedrun timer inaccurately keeping track of the time passed.
Fixed a bug where the 1-2 bgm could play in the world map.
