In Development

Have You Ever Wanted Movies Free?

Wega And The Lost Media is an in development shitpost 2D Platformer that follows the iconic Luigi and Mario recolors Wega and Urio, and their quest to stop the evil Maltigi from making their world LOST MEDIA!
The game will contain 8 chapters of content, 4 weapons for you to break out of bounds with, a soundtrack that's probably better than this game deserves, and shoe bench.

Story

One day, after discussing their latest attempt to sell Frees Movies, Wega and Urio were interupted by Maltigi breaking in to steal them all! Now its up to the Movie Free bros to save their media, and the world!

Along the way, you'll meet unique characters and explore strange locations, from an empire run by people named Todd, to an island obsessed with the retro 80's, Wega and Urio will have to jump, dive, and hammer-swing their way into finding the lost media!
