copyright infringement and you

Recently, a notable wega fangame, Wegas challenge: The Idol, was DMCA struck off the Roblox platform.
To avoid also being DMCAed by The Big N, a few things will have to be changed from now and demo 3.
There will be no MAJOR changes in gameplay or design.
Some images of Nintendo characters will be removed/replaced to avoid copyright, and some unoriginal music tracks will be slightly altered.
While this is a fan game, and has been advertised as such, we would like to see it completed and not DMCAed off the internet!
