How much more game do you have to make

Originally, on the Team Lost Media website, you might have noticed this little paragraph,

While MOST of the stuff in this is still true, we miiiiiiiiiight have jumped the gun on announcing the world count.
To get to the point - Wega And The Lost Media will have 6 worlds, not 8.

but why are you giving me LESS content how COULD you

It's hard to be more specific about WHY we cut down on the world count without Spoilering The Entire Game, but heres the basics of it:
When coming up for the world count back in 2024, we had clear-cut ideas for (almost) each world.
World 1 would be a grasslands-mountain, World 2 would be a desert, World 3 would be a You Cant Hear That Yet Thats Spoilers, and so on.
However, we never actually came up with a good idea for World 4, and ended up putting all our good ideas into worlds 5 and beyond...

So we shot World 4 dead and moved World 5 to where World 4 would have been.

this still doesnt explain why theres 6 worlds and not 7

Now this is where the spoilers part comes in...
To atempt to explain, our ideas for (post world 4 removal) Worlds 6 and 7 were very similar.
So, we decided to merge them into one final world, making the total world count 6.

So what have you been working on then?

Currently, we're working on the next two updates to WATLM. One of these is the World 2 Update, which will contain World 2 and its boss.
The other update is a patch to Demo 1, which we're calling the Demo 1.5 Update!

The most notable features of Demo 1.5 are Save Files, Multiplayer, Playable Urio, and Bonus Levels!

What are "Bonus Levels"?

Bonus levels are levels not connected to any world. They're optional bonuses that you can play at any time!
We currently have one bonus level in development for Demo 1.5 (but could probably do another, we have a few ideas).
